About SVMC Energy & Smart Infra Pvt Ltd

Specialized Project Company for Diverse Sectors

SVMC Energy was established in 2018 with a vision to transform the distributed energy market of India. Our founders have been working in the Engineering, Procurement and Construction (EPC) business across the energy sector for more than decade. Our founders have executed projects of more than 40,000 crores in Oil and Gas, Petrochemical and Renewables, with more than 100+ Solar Rooftop projects spread across 16 states in India. In these last few years the founders have realised that 2 main problems plague the distributed energy sector - Financial Risk and Technical Risk which are stopping the MSME customers from joining the wave of energy transition.

Our team comprises highly qualified alumni from IITs/IIMs, bringing a wealth of experience exceeding 25 years across our service domains. We are committed to providing end-to-end support, partnering with our clients from the initial project design stage and pre-bid processes, through meticulous planning, engineering, procurement, flawless execution, and final handover.

Our holistic approach ensures each project is optimized not only for design but also for execution efficiency, long-term durability, and operational excellence, thereby creating superior value for money. We believe in transforming tomorrow through innovative and sustainable solutions.

Our Project Showcase

LED Street Light Installation

We have successfully installed and are currently maintaining over 4 Lakh LED street lights for a Government of India PSU across six states and fifteen cities. This large-scale project highlights our expertise in public infrastructure implementation, operations & maintenance (O&M), and collaboration with government bodies.

Our smart LED installation integrates energy-efficient lighting with advanced technology for remote control, automation, and features such as scheduling, dimming, and colour adjustments. This ensures optimal lighting efficiency, convenience, and ambiance, playing a key role in urban sustainability and energy conservation.

We are also proud to be part of major projects like the Gwalior Smart City LED Project and the Maharashtra LED Project, where we have been enhancing safety, visibility, and energy efficiency. In addition, we have installed 40 EV chargers in seven cities, supporting the transition to sustainable transportation. By integrating electric vehicle infrastructure, we’re helping reduce dependence on fossil fuels and facilitating the shift to cleaner energy solutions.

Ground Mounted & Rooftop Solar

We specialize in providing sustainable solar energy solutions across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, helping clients reduce their dependence on non-renewable energy sources. With over 500 solar panels installed across five cities, our efforts are making a significant contribution to creating a greener energy landscape.

As an MNRE channel partner for Rosthul, we offer end-to-end solar consultancy, guiding clients through government subsidy schemes like PM Kusum and Mukh Bigh Yojna. Our expertise spans both ground-mounted and rooftop solar projects, where we manage everything from site surveys and soil testing to design engineering, procurement, installation, and commissioning.

Notable projects include solar installations on asbestos rooftops, transmission bays, transformer yards, and control rooms, all ensuring reliable, efficient, and sustainable energy solutions. Our work on the Maharashtra Solar Project, alongside the Maharashtra LED Project, reflects our commitment to a comprehensive approach to energy efficiency, helping the state transition to a greener and more resilient energy future.

Smart Meter Installation & O&M

We're transforming energy management through the deployment of smart meters across multiple states and cities, enabling real-time monitoring and data-driven energy usage. These intelligent systems promote efficiency, cost savings, and a smarter, more sustainable energy infrastructure.

With over 1800 smart meters installed across nine states, we bring deep expertise in both installation and operations & maintenance (O&M). Our process covers everything—from meter replacement and connectivity setup to seamless data integration. These smart meters empower users with real-time insights and utilities with tools for grid optimisation and performance enhancement.

We manage the entire lifecycle of smart metering projects—ensuring smooth deployment, reliable functionality, and continuous support. With features like automated reporting and remote connectivity, our smart metering solutions are helping utility providers and consumers alike embrace a more efficient, future-ready energy ecosystem.

EV Charging Stations & Infra

We’ve successfully facilitated the installation of over 100 EV charging stations across seven cities, supporting India’s transition to sustainable transportation. Our work enables convenient, fast, and reliable electric vehicle recharging, helping reduce dependence on fossil fuels.

From infrastructure setup and electrical wiring to charger integration and system deployment, we cover every aspect of EV charging installation. Our end-to-end support ensures that both public and private entities can expand their EV networks confidently and seamlessly, driving forward the future of clean mobility.
