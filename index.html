About SVMC Energy

Specialized in Energy & Smart Infrastructure Projects

SVMC Energy & Smart Infra Pvt Ltd is a project company specializing in serving Government Organizations, PSUs, Nagar Nigam, and Private Sectors. Our team comprises highly qualified alumni with over 25 years of rich experience.

We offer end-to-end support from inception to handover, focusing on detailed engineering, vendor management, site management, and supply chain optimization to deliver higher value.

Consultancy & PMU

Providing expert consultancy, Project Management Unit (PMU) services, and business development strategies for energy projects.

Project Implementation & Execution

End-to-end project execution including detailed engineering, site management, and technical support.

Electrical & Smart Infra Work

Expertise in LT/HT Infra, line shifting, smart meter installation, and electrical contracting.

EV Consultancy & Infrastructure

Consultancy for EV charging infrastructure, battery swapping, fleet management, and OEM support.

Solar Projects (Ground & Rooftop)

Operations & Maintenance
